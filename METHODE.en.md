# Method

[← Index](README.en.md)

This document is not a styled opinion. It is the product of a research protocol, and being transparent about that protocol is part of its reliability.

## How it was produced

Five deep research campaigns were run on 14 and 15 June 2026, mobilizing on the order of seventy web research agents working in parallel, each assigned a precise angle (chips and foundries, compute and energy, frontier models, open source, geopolitics, lock-in and shutdown, European policy, robotics, climate, the economic bubble, quantum, space, and the perspective of Hugging Face).

Three principles structured the work:

1. **Adversarial verification.** Every high-stakes claim was handed to a separate agent tasked not with confirming it but with refuting it. A claim was kept only if it survived that attempt at demolition. Five claims were corrected through this mechanism (see below).

2. **Counter-current thinking.** A dedicated agent, called the lateral one, was tasked with producing the non-obvious scenarios, the black swans and the reversals that the main angles risked missing, precisely to counter the framing bias of the inquiry on itself.

3. **Systematic dating and sourcing.** Figures are dated. Sources are listed in [`SOURCES.md`](SOURCES.md).

Analytical lenses (Yuk Hui's technodiversity, Yanis Varoufakis's technofeudalism) were used to frame the thinking internally. They are not cited as authorities in the text: the document's claims rest on the verified sources, not on these lenses.

Finally, the central verdict was submitted to a fusion of three families of models (Opus 4.8, GPT-5.5 and Gemini 3.1 Pro) answering blind and independently. All three converged on the same framework, which strengthens confidence in it. The residual divergences, notably the stance to take on ASML, were arbitrated in favor of the best-sourced answers.

## The corrections owned

Several spectacular claims were corrected along the way. Naming them is a way of protecting oneself from oneself.

- **Mistral's "lag" on reasoning.** Mistral Large 3's score of 43.9% on the GPQA Diamond benchmark is obtained in standard mode, with reasoning disabled. This is not a structural limit: the reasoning variants largely close the gap. The document therefore does not present this lag as a permanent handicap.

- **Open-weight as the "only" path to compliance.** For regulated sectors, self-hosted open-weight is not the only legally compliant architecture, it is one of four. Self-hosting is a matter of sovereignty and cost, not an exclusive legal obligation.

- **The Health Data Hub contract.** Scaleway won it through a tender in 2026, ending the Azure hosting. But the value of the contract was not made public (the figure of 20 million euros sometimes cited is unsupported), and no source confirms that the EDPB intends to make it a reference case.

- **The cost of French electricity.** The real wholesale cost is on the order of 60 to 67 dollars per megawatt-hour (RTE data), not the 44 dollars sometimes put forward. The French advantage exists, but it is more modest than the myth.

- **A public claim circulating about Mistral's revenue** (the share earned outside France) was deliberately left out of the text, for lack of a verifiable primary source. The document prefers to omit an unverifiable figure rather than propagate it.

## The debates left open

On some subjects, the best sources contradict one another head-on, and it would have been dishonest to settle the matter.

- **The bursting of the AI bubble.** The most serious analysts are in direct disagreement on the central facts (AI's share of US GDP growth, valuations relative to the 2000 peak, the real lifespan of GPUs). No one has reliable timing. The document gives the ranges and the disagreements.

- **AI's water consumption.** Estimates vary by a factor of a thousand depending on the scope chosen. Any figure quoted about water is to be handled with caution.

- **Quantum timelines.** The industrial roadmaps are markedly more optimistic than the skeptical literature. The document distinguishes everywhere between the demonstrated and the promised.

## Limits

Figures from March to June 2026 drawn from aggregators are flagged as such and remain to be reconfirmed against a primary source before any public attributed citation. Space and quantum were added in a later pass. This document is a personal research effort, not an institutional opinion.
